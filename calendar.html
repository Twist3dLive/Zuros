Zuros has a 16 month calendar years, with each of the 16 months being named after the original 16 gods and goddesses that conqured Zuros from the primordials. Each month is 4 weeks long with each week consistind of 8 days. The days in Zuros are always a little bit longer than our own, lasting 32 hours with, 16 hours of daylight, and 16 hours of night. It is said that the days and nights used to incredibly different when dragons were the primary species across Zurs, however it is said that it changed when Inir (God, of the Sun) and Yros (Goddess, of the Moon) were approached by the new god Kiten (God, of Knowledge and Science) who proposed that this order of the day and night cycle would be both better for the gods and mortals on Zuros.
